Basil Sauce

Description

A simple but tasteful sauce for your pasta

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves , crushed
  • 24 oz/800g fresh cherry tomatoes halved
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil lightly packed

Method

  1. Step 1 Cook 1lb/500g of dried spaghetti (or any other long dried pasta) in a large pot of salted boiling water per packet MINUS 1 minute.
  2. Step 2 Heat oil over medium high heat. Add garlic and cook until fragrant, add tomato and cook briefly, just to warm through. Toss with pasta and cooking water per Base Directions. Toss through basil, serve with parmesan
  3. Step 3 When pasta is ready, scoop out 1 cup of pasta cooking water, and drain pasta.
  4. Step 4 Add pasta into chosen sauce with 3/4 cup pasta cooking water. Toss pasta until sauce thickens and sticks to pasta (called "emulsifying" the sauce), 1 - 2 minutes. Use more pasta water if needed to loosen sauce.
  5. Step 5 Season with salt and pepper to taste. Enjoy!
